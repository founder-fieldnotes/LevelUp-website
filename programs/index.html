Program Layer

Programs translate strategy into applied work.

LevelUp programs include workshops, cohorts, institutional training, advisory work, accelerators, and labs. Each format is designed to strengthen capability while staying connected to the wider economic systems agenda.

Program Role

Programs create the practical operating layer where partners, learners, founders, and institutions turn strategy into visible progress.

Who It Is For

Institutions, employers, funders, and ecosystem builders.

Programs are built for partners who need more than content delivery: they need capability, confidence, implementation structure, and visible outcomes.

What It Includes

Curriculum, facilitation, applied projects, and reporting logic.

Engagements can include program design, instructor support, employer input, project briefs, assessment rubrics, cohort operations, and partner-ready reporting.

Outcomes

Capability people can use and stakeholders can see.

The goal is practical progress: stronger talent signals, better employer alignment, more credible projects, and clearer evidence for funders and institutions.

Engagement Formats

Workshops, cohorts, advisory sprints, accelerators, and labs.

LevelUp can work through focused sessions, multi-week cohorts, institutional training, strategy engagements, or venture-building formats.
