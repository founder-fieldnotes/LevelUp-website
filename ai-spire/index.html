AI.SPIRE

AI.SPIRE is the first course launched under Darb.Tech: a 24-week intensive journey into Python for Artificial Intelligence, from data processing to machine learning. The current pilot includes 51 learners and is delivered with Future Skills Fund and Istidama Consulting.

Focus on

Partner roles, learner or employer relevance, and what is being built over time.

Read for

Signals of durable capacity rather than short-term activity alone.

Initiative partners

AI.SPIRE is delivered through a coordinated partner model.

LevelUp contributes curriculum design, delivery expertise, and systems strategy alongside local operating and funding partners.

AI.SPIRE
Future Skills Fund
Istidama Consulting
Partners

LevelUp Economy, Istidama Consulting, and Future Skills Fund.

AI.SPIRE brings together curriculum and delivery expertise, local operational capacity, and funding support through the Crown Prince Foundation ecosystem in Jordan.

Current Pilot

51 learners in a 24-week applied AI pathway.

The pilot combines Python for AI, data processing, machine learning foundations, English-language delivery, soft skills, and hands-on project work.

Audience

Learners, employers, founders, and institutions.

Capstone projects are designed to solve employer needs, create venture pathways, and help participants apply AI to real economic problems.

Delivery Model

How AI.SPIRE moves from training to economic capability.

LevelUp provides expertise in curriculum development, program design, and delivery while guiding the wider systems approach around AI capability, entrepreneurship, and employer adoption.

Phase 01

Build technical foundations

Learners develop Python, data processing, and AI fundamentals through structured English-language instruction.

Phase 02

Apply AI to real problems

Project-based learning gives participants hands-on practice with machine learning, problem-solving, teamwork, and communication.

Phase 03

Connect employers and ventures

Employer needs and market opportunities shape capstones that can lead toward jobs, internal innovation, or new company formation.

Phase 04

Strengthen the wider ecosystem

The initiative connects applied AI learning to startup incubation, corporate development, and institutional capacity-building.

Activities

Hybrid, project-based AI training

Instructors from the United States and Jordan support a practical program built around Python for AI, English fluency, soft skills, and applied outputs.

Outcomes

Global readiness and local relevance

The program prepares learners for global opportunities while grounding their work in employer needs, venture creation, and Jordan’s innovation ecosystem.

Related Resource

The LevelUp model
