Digital Forensics Support Instructor

The Digital Forensics Support Instructor supports evidence-safe practice, forensic lab execution, timeline analysis, reporting, and learner coaching for the digital forensics pathway.

Track Focus

This role helps learners understand evidence handling, defensible analysis, documentation, and the bridge between investigation and incident response.

What You Support

Forensics labs, evidence habits, and analysis workflows.

Help learners practice chain of custody, imaging concepts, artifact analysis, timelines, memory forensics, mobile workflows, and clear reporting.

What You Do

Make technical analysis defensible and teachable.

Support lab setup, reinforce evidence-safe habits, guide analysis, coordinate with TAs, and help learners produce decision-ready reports.

What Success Looks Like

Learners can handle evidence with care and communicate findings clearly.

Graduates understand forensic discipline, validation, documentation, and how investigative work supports cybersecurity and organizational decisions.

Candidate Profile

We are looking for forensic judgment, lab confidence, and learner-centered support.

Strong candidates can keep practice evidence-safe, explain investigative reasoning, and help learners build confidence without lowering standards.
