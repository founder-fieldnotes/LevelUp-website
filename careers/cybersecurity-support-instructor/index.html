Cybersecurity Support Instructor

The Cybersecurity Support Instructor owns applied lab support, track execution, technical reinforcement, and learner coaching for the cybersecurity pathway.

Best fit

People who can teach, support, and execute with rigor.

What matters

Clear ownership, strong judgment, and practical delivery habits.

What You Support

Cybersecurity labs and applied track delivery.

Help learners practice networking, recon, web attacks, exploitation workflows, Active Directory concepts, reporting, and defensive visibility.

What You Do

Make technical practice clear, safe, and useful.

Support demos, debug labs, reinforce concepts, coordinate with TAs, and help translate activity into employer-relevant artifacts.

What Success Looks Like

Learners can explain what they did and why it matters.

Graduates demonstrate practical skill, documentation habits, responsible AI use, and awareness of detection and remediation logic.

Candidate Profile

We are looking for technical fluency and patient instructional presence.

Strong candidates can run applied cybersecurity practice, help learners get unstuck, and maintain standards across hybrid delivery.
