Teaching Assistant

Teaching Assistants provide the daily support layer that makes a rigorous hybrid program work: learner troubleshooting, lab reinforcement, grading support, and early visibility into learner needs.

Support Layer

This role is essential to learner confidence, hybrid parity, and smooth coordination between instructors, cohorts, and program leadership.

What You Support

Daily learner progress and lab readiness.

Help learners set up environments, complete labs, understand feedback, document work, and stay on track through difficult technical material.

What You Do

Keep the program responsive and humane.

Answer questions, surface recurring blockers, support grading workflows, and help instructors see where learners need more structure.

What Success Looks Like

Learners feel supported while standards stay high.

The best TA work improves completion, confidence, assessment quality, and early identification of learner risk.

Candidate Profile

We are looking for patient, organized technical supporters.

Strong candidates are reliable, curious, clear in communication, and comfortable helping learners work through ambiguity.
