Join The Work

Build programs that prepare people for real technical work.

LevelUp is recruiting instructional and support talent for cybersecurity and digital forensics delivery. These roles are designed for people who can combine technical credibility, learner support, documentation discipline, and high-quality program execution.

Read for

Responsibility, decision ownership, and the learner or partner experience.

Best fit

People who care about execution quality as much as subject-matter depth.

Open Roles

Cybersecurity and digital forensics delivery team.

Each role has a LevelUp careers page with the Amman Cyber Plan role brief linked as supporting program context.

Instructional Leadership

Lead Instructor

Owns instructional standards, program coherence, selected concept sessions, and quality across the full cyber and digital forensics delivery model.

Cybersecurity Support Instructor

What We Value

Technical seriousness with learner empathy.

The strongest candidates can explain complex topics clearly, support learners patiently, and maintain high standards without turning delivery into gatekeeping.

Delivery Culture

Documentation, feedback, and follow-through.

Program quality depends on clear communication, consistent assessment, visible learner support, and strong coordination across hybrid delivery environments.

Ways In

Instructional, advisory, and project-based roles.

LevelUp will continue to add opportunities across delivery, partnerships, research, product, and ecosystem-building as the work expands.

LevelUp designs holistic systems that connect talent, innovation, capital, employer demand, and institutions.
