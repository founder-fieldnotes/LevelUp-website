Darb.Tech

Darb.Tech is the program platform for applied technical pathways in Jordan, beginning with AI.SPIRE and expanding next into cybersecurity and digital forensics.

Challenge / Opportunity

The goal is not simply to launch courses. Darb.Tech can become a vehicle for talent systems, employer capability, startup incubation, and applied work that supports economic growth.

First Course

AI.SPIRE launches applied AI capability.

The first Darb.Tech pathway is a 24-week Python for AI program combining technical training, English, soft skills, and project-based learning.

Next Pathway

Cybersecurity and digital forensics in Amman.

The next program can build on the Amman cybersecurity plan, with hybrid delivery, employer relevance, and hands-on technical labs.

Economic Role

A platform for skills, employers, and ventures.

Darb.Tech connects learner capability to capstones, employer needs, startup ideas, and future institutional partnerships.

Platform Logic

How Darb.Tech turns programs into a wider talent system.

LevelUp helps shape curriculum, delivery, and ecosystem strategy so Darb.Tech pathways create value beyond learner completion.

Phase 01

Launch applied AI

Use AI.SPIRE to build technical capability and prove the model through real projects, hybrid delivery, and English-language readiness.

Phase 02

Bring employers into the design

Use employer needs to shape curriculum, capstones, and practical outputs that create stronger hiring and innovation signals.

Phase 03

Expand into cyber

Build the next Darb.Tech pathway around cybersecurity and digital forensics for Amman’s employer and institutional needs.

Phase 04

Support ventures and corporate adoption

Turn capstones into company ideas, employer solutions, internal AI adoption work, and stronger regional innovation capacity.

Activities

Applied technical programs and capstones

Darb.Tech gives learners structured pathways to build, present, and apply technical capability to real-world problems.

Outcomes

Employer relevance and venture potential

The platform is designed to lead toward employment, stronger employer capability, and new ventures emerging from project work.
