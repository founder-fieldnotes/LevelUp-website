Future Skills Fund

Future Skills Fund is the funding mechanism and operational oversight layer supporting the current AI.SPIRE pilot of 51 learners through the Crown Prince Foundation ecosystem in Jordan.

Focus on

Partner roles, learner or employer relevance, and what is being built over time.

Read for

Signals of durable capacity rather than short-term activity alone.

Funding and delivery ecosystem

Future Skills Fund anchors AI.SPIRE inside a wider Jordan skills agenda.

The initiative connects funding, oversight, local delivery alignment, and applied AI program design.

Funding and oversight
Flagship initiative
Local delivery partner
Funding

Capital that supports capability-building.

FSF enables the program to focus on durable outcomes rather than one-off training activity.

Oversight

Operational clarity and accountability.

The funder role helps align stakeholders, track delivery, and keep the 51-learner pilot connected to larger goals.

Ecosystem

A bridge into Jordan’s youth and skills agenda.

Through the Crown Prince Foundation ecosystem, FSF connects AI.SPIRE to a broader national focus on youth opportunity and economic participation.

Funding Logic

FSF turns capital into coordinated implementation.

In the LevelUp model, funding is most powerful when it strengthens the whole system: curriculum, delivery, employer relevance, reporting, and future pathways.

Phase 01

Set the mandate

Clarify what the initiative needs to accomplish for learners, employers, institutions, and the wider economy.

Phase 02

Support delivery architecture

Fund the operating model needed for curriculum development, hybrid delivery, learner support, and partner coordination.

Phase 03

Maintain accountability

Use oversight and reporting to keep implementation visible, credible, and aligned with stakeholder expectations.

Phase 04

Create a platform for future initiatives

Use what is learned from AI.SPIRE to inform future programs, including cybersecurity and other Darb.Tech pathways.

Activities

Funding and operational oversight

FSF supports the conditions that allow partners to design, deliver, measure, and improve the initiative.

Outcomes

Stronger public confidence

Clear funder involvement helps learners, employers, and institutions understand that the work is serious and accountable.
