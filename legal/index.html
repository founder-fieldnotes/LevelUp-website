Governance Layer

Legal and policy information for LevelUp Economy.

These working policies explain how visitors may use the site, how LevelUp handles basic contact information, and how to reach us about privacy, accessibility, or website questions.

Important Note

This language is written for launch readiness and should be reviewed by counsel before the site becomes the primary production domain.

Privacy Notice

Information we collect

LevelUp Economy may collect information that you choose to provide through contact, partnership, funding, subscribe, or careers forms. This may include your name, email address, organization, role, areas of interest, and the contents of your message. We may also receive basic technical information from standard web hosting, analytics, security, or email systems, such as browser type, device information, referring pages, and approximate usage patterns.

  • We use submitted information to respond to inquiries, manage partnerships, share requested updates, evaluate role interest, and improve the website and public resources.
  • We do not sell personal information. We do not use contact form submissions for unrelated advertising without consent.
  • We may share information with service providers that help operate the website, email, analytics, forms, security, or future CRM workflows, but only for legitimate business purposes.
  • You may request that we update, correct, or delete contact information you previously provided by emailing info@levelupeconomy.com.
Terms of Use

Using this website

The LevelUp Economy website is provided for general informational purposes. Site content, frameworks, case studies, graphics, and resources may not be copied, redistributed, modified, or used commercially without prior written permission, except where ordinary linking, quotation, or fair use applies.

  • Public content is not legal, financial, investment, or professional advice.
  • Links to third-party websites are provided for convenience and do not mean LevelUp controls or endorses those websites.
  • Users may not interfere with site operation, attempt unauthorized access, scrape the site at unreasonable scale, or misuse contact forms.
  • LevelUp may update website content, policies, pages, programs, and resources at any time.
Accessibility

Inclusive access commitment

LevelUp Economy aims to make this website usable for a wide range of visitors and is working toward WCAG 2.2 AA alignment. We design for keyboard navigation, meaningful headings, sufficient contrast, responsive layouts, descriptive links, and reduced reliance on motion.

  • If you experience an accessibility barrier, email info@levelupeconomy.com with the page URL, the issue you encountered, and the assistive technology or device you used if relevant.
  • We will use accessibility feedback to prioritize fixes and improve future templates.
  • Some third-party links, embedded services, or externally hosted documents may be outside LevelUp’s direct control.
Contact

Questions about these policies

For privacy, accessibility, website usage, or legal questions, contact LevelUp Economy at info@levelupeconomy.com. Include enough detail for us to understand the request and the page or interaction involved.
