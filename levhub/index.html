Phase 2 Vision

LevHub is not a traditional LMS. It is a learning, building, and ecosystem intelligence platform.

In Phase 1, this page explains the product direction. In Phase 2, it becomes the bridge into authenticated experiences for learners, instructors, TAs, partners, and program managers.

What It Solves
  • Applied work Support real project workflows instead of generic e-learning patterns.
  • GitHub-native delivery Surface repos, pull requests, CI, and review context in one place.
  • Operational visibility Improve grading, reporting, cohort health, and partner-facing evidence.

Product Framing

The point is not to rebuild a generic LMS. The point is to support the workflows that generic LMS products fail to support.

That means GitHub-native assignments, flexible scoring, cohort operations, reporting quality, and visibility across programs and outcomes.
